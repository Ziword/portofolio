Weights App

This application is created using VB.NET and serves as a tool for recording incoming and outgoing vehicles at a batching plant, fulfilling the administrative needs of Merak Jaya Beton company. Its main purpose is to facilitate the process of vehicle registration, ensuring proper documentation of each vehicle's entry and exit.
