Weights App

This application was created using VB.NET and is designed for recording the weight of vehicles before entering or leaving a batching plant. It is used for administrative purposes at Merak Jaya Beton company. The application also includes features such as a connection to the weighing machine, automatic updates from the server, and real-time streaming from CCTV cameras located in the plant.
